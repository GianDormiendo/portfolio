About Me

Academic focus and research philosophy

I am a STEM-focused high school student with a strong academic orientation toward physics, mathematics, and engineering, driven by a deep interest in understanding complex systems and translating theory into practical solutions.

My work spans research, robotics, and computational projects, with particular emphasis on data-driven decision-making, system optimization, and technology for environmental and social impact. I am especially interested in how mathematical models, physical principles, and intelligent algorithms can be combined to address real-world problems such as disaster mitigation, infrastructure resilience, and resource management.

I approach learning with a research mindset—prioritizing first principles, analytical rigor, and clarity of reasoning over surface-level results. Whether working on a prototype, a model, or a written study, I value precision, reproducibility, and meaningful impact.
